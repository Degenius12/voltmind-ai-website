## Brand Overview

### Brand Identity
VoltMind AI represents the intersection of **intelligence** and **energy**. Our brand combines concepts of neural processing with electrical power to convey:

- **Innovation**: Cutting-edge AI technology
- **Power**: Energy and capability
- **Intelligence**: Thoughtful, mind-driven solutions
- **Reliability**: Professional, enterprise-grade service

### Brand Personality
- **Professional** yet **approachable**
- **Innovative** yet **trustworthy**
- **Technical** yet **user-friendly**
- **Bold** yet **sophisticated**

## Logo Don'ts

❌ **Do NOT**:
- Stretch or distort the logo
- Change logo colors
- Add effects (shadows, glows, outlines)
- Rotate the logo
- Recreate or redraw the logo
- Place logo on busy backgrounds without sufficient contrast
- Use logo smaller than 32px in height

✅ **Do**:
- Maintain aspect ratio when scaling
- Use provided logo files
- Ensure adequate contrast with background
- Provide clear space around logo

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors

#### Navy (Dark Background)
- **Hex**: `#0F172A`
- **RGB**: 15, 23, 42
- **Use**: Backgrounds, headers, dark sections

#### Electric Blue (Brand Accent)
- **Hex**: `#2563EB`
- **RGB**: 37, 99, 235
- **Use**: CTAs, links, accents, highlights

#### White
- **Hex**: `#FFFFFF`
- **RGB**: 255, 255, 255
- **Use**: Text on dark backgrounds, clean sections

### Secondary Colors

#### Slate Gray
- **Hex**: `#64748B`
- **RGB**: 100, 116, 139
- **Use**: Secondary text, borders, subtle elements

#### Light Gray (Backgrounds)
- **Hex**: `#F8FAFC`
- **RGB**: 248, 250, 252
- **Use**: Light section backgrounds, cards

### Gradient (Optional)

**Electric Blue to Purple**
- From: `#2563EB` (Electric Blue)
- To: `#7C3AED` (Purple)
- Use sparingly for special emphasis, CTAs, or visual accents

## Color Usage Guidelines

### Backgrounds
- **Dark sections**: Navy (`#0F172A`)
- **Light sections**: White or Light Gray (`#F8FAFC`)

### Text
- **Primary text on light**: Navy (`#0F172A`)
- **Primary text on dark**: White (`#FFFFFF`)
- **Secondary text**: Slate Gray (`#64748B`)

### Interactive Elements
- **Primary CTA**: Electric Blue (`#2563EB`)
- **Links**: Electric Blue (`#2563EB`)
- **Hover states**: Darker shade of Electric Blue

## Typography

### Primary Font: Inter

**Headings**:
- Font: Inter
- Weights: 600 (SemiBold), 700 (Bold)
- Style: Clean, modern, professional

**Body Text**:
- Font: Inter
- Weights: 400 (Regular), 500 (Medium)
- Style: Readable, neutral

### Font Sizes (Web)

- **H1**: 48-60px (Desktop), 36-42px (Mobile)
- **H2**: 36-48px (Desktop), 30-36px (Mobile)
- **H3**: 24-30px
- **H4**: 20-24px
- **Body**: 16-18px
- **Small**: 14px

### Line Height
- Headings: 1.2–1.3
- Body text: 1.6–1.8
